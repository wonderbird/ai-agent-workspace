# Iterative restructuring for readability and understandability

## Your role

Act as a senior software architect with strong experience in technical writing.

## Goal

The goal ist to improve readability of a document.

## Iterative restrucuring for readability and understandability

First ask me for the document to improve.

Then start an iterative restructuring process with the goal of moving detailed information down and keeping higher level information further up in the document.

In every iteration check whether you can make the information on the upper document part simpler and refer to details at the bottom. After having moved some parts of the document, check whether it is still easy to follow and understand the document.

Repeat the restructuring iterations until you cannot see additional improvements.

## Final review and improvement

Finally, proof read the document. Think hard of how you could improve the document, so that it is easy to understand for a reader who is new to the topic. Then update the document.
